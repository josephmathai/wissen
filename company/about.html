Wissen Technology: A Company Par Excellence

About

Wissen Technology, an affiliate company of Wissen Infotech, is a high-end, specialized technology firm focused on Banking & Finance, Telecom and Healthcare domains, offering Application Development, Analytics, and Digital Experience services in over 5 countries. We are one of the fastest growing companies in the industry, thanks to our people who, at all levels, are passionate technology leaders. Our uniqueness comes from our focus on excellence, especially when it comes to the quality of our services and people. Our consultants across business verticals and practice areas are highly skilled, very motivated and very client focused. Such commitment to clients has resulted in excellent, strategic relationships with Fortune 500 companies and 90% repeat business.
