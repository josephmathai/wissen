Who are we

Wissen Technology, an affiliate of Wissen Infotech, is a specialized technology practice for high end consulting primarily geared towards the domains of Banking & Finance, Telecom, and Healthcare. We believe that the technology and thought leadership that we command in the industry is the direct result of the kind of people we have been able to attract, to form this organization. We are committed to providing them the best possible experience and career, which extends to providing the best possible experience and value to our clients, who are typically the Fortune 100 leaders in their respective industry.
